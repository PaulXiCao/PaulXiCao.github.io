The tiptoi pen is a wonderful toy for small children.
For new books one needs to copy audio files to the pen. 
There is a docking station for newer models that takes care of this, a synchronization app for Windows, but on linux one needs to download and copy them manually.

The files can be found on [this webpage](https://service.ravensburger.de/tiptoi%C2%AE/tiptoi%C2%AE_Audiodateien/Audiodateien_tiptoi%C2%AE_B%C3%BCcher) (just search for the book title).
Copy them next to the already present files on the stick, e.g. `cp new_book_file.gme /mnt/tiptoi_device/tiptoi/`.
