mounting already encrypted folders:


sudo mount -t ecryptfs <folder> <folder> -o key=passphrase,ecryptfs_cipher=aes,ecryptfs_key_bytes=32,ecryptfs_passthrough=yes,ecryptfs_enable_filename_crypto=yes
