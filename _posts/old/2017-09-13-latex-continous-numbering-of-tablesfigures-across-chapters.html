--- title: 'Latex: continuous numbering of tables/figures across chapters' date: 2017-09-13 06:28:46.000000000 +02:00 tags: - latex - numbering ---

put this in your preamble

[code language="latex"]
\usepackage{chngcntr}
\counterwithout{figure}{chapter}
\counterwithout{table}{chapter}
[/code]
