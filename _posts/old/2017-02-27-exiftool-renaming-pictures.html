--- title: 'exiftool: renaming pictures' date: 2017-02-27 22:53:09.000000000 +01:00 categories: - linux tags: - bash - cmdline - exiftool - picture - renaming ---

rename pictures according to their exifdata time stemp:

[code]
exiftool "-FileName<CreateDate" -d "%Y%m%d_%H%M%S_%%c.%%e" .
[/code]
