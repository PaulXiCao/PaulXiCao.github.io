--- title: turn num lock on at startup date: 2016-06-22 11:45:12.000000000 +02:00 categories: - linux tags: - gui - mint ---

install numlockx and edit config:

vim /etc/mdm/Init/Default

if [ -x /usr/bin/numlockx ]; then
/usr/bin/numlockx on
fi
