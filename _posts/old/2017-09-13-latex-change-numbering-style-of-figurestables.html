--- title: 'Latex: change numbering style of figures/tables' date: 2017-09-13 06:31:16.000000000 +02:00 categories: - latex tags: - numbering ---

put this in your preamble

[code language="latex"]
% figures/tables should be labeled: 1,2,3,4,...
\renewcommand{\thefigure}{\arabic{figure}}
\renewcommand{\thetable}{\arabic{table}}
[/code]
